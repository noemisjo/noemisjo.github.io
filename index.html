Stray records [Featured project]

Format: Live sound and video performance Duration: 45'Format: flute, tapes, SD PAL Synopsis: “Stray Records” is an archive-based project that brings unused raw footage from Noemi Sjöberg’s stay in New York in 2001 into dialogue with a re-reading of audio tapes documenting concerts, rehearsals, and recording sessions by Barbara Held. The project is presented through images displayed on CRT monitors, using a live SD camera and projector.
