Continuous change (2021)

Format and size: Cyanotype on gelatin emulsion on ground aluminium/ 100cm x 50 cm x 0,5mm

Description: "Continuous change" describes the idea of a union. Each generation melts into another, we are part of a cycle. Every human being has a connection to another. We merge with the other to become one. The work represents the young mother, grandmother and child and is made of the symbiosis of cyanotype with aluminium. We all come from the same place and we are in continuous change.
